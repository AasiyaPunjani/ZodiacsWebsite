Leo (23rd July - 22nd August)

Leo

2023 Horoscope for Leo

SYMBOL: The Lion

ARCHETYPE: The Creative

ELEMENT: Fire

MODE: Fixed

HOUSE: 5th House of Pleasure

RULER: The Sun, ruler of our core personality

DUALITY: Yang

CHARACTERISTICS: Ruled by the centre of our solar system, the equally fiery Leo is the star of the show and the lover of the limelight. They like to roar bright and loud, great performers, they can be like the regal king themselves- generous servers but also the leaders of their kingdom. While they may be self-centred, their main ruse to gain adoration is to spread joy and charm, entertaining others and bringing sunny happiness all round. They are confident, extravagant (in gestures and money), loyal, and ambitious.
