THE ZODIACS

The zodiacs are 12 constellations that exist in the Zodiac Belt. As the Earth rotates around the sun, we are able to see different constellations. Depending on where the Earth is when you are born, you can identify your zodiac sign, which can tell you about your personality, characteristics, and horoscope.
